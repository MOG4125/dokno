How to finalize in Koala:
  1. Press PROG SELECT + Pad 1 on your physical keyboard to load changes.
  2. Open Koala ➔ Settings ➔ MIDI ➔ Map MIDI.
  3. Turn your latch knob left ➔ Tap LOOP ➔ Turn knob right.
  4. Turn knob left ➔ Tap HOLD ➔ Turn knob right.
